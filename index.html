About JREC
ORGANISATION

The JREC is constituted as an independent ethics committee through the Deanery of University of Zimbabwe Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences (UZ-CHS) and the Parirenyatwa Group of Hospitals.

Read More

Our Vision

To be an effective Institutional Research Ethics Committee.

Our Mission

To assist students and members of the UZ-CHS and Parirenyatwa Group of Hospitals to conduct scientific, ethical and relevant research

To regulate and approve the conduct of health research in The University of Zimbabwe Faculty of Medicine of Health Sciences and the Parirenyatwa Group of Hospitals
